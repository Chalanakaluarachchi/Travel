Sacred Places

A place of worship became sacred and suitable by virtue of the holy's appearing at that place. Sacred places were also sites of natural and historical significance for the community: springs, river crossings, threshing places, trees or groves… In ceremonial object: Places of worship and sacrifice.

Nallur Kovil

Nallur Kandaswamy Kovil is a significant Hindu temple, located in Nallur, Northern Province, Sri Lanka.

Sri Dalada Maligawa

The Temple Sacred Tooth Relic or Sri Dalada Maligawa which houses the relic of the tooth of the Buddha

Isurumuniya Rajamaha Viharaya

Isurumuniya is a Buddhist temple situated near to the Tissa Wewa in Anuradhapura, Sri Lanka. There are four carvings of special interest in this Vihara. They are the Isurumuniya Lovers, Elephant Pond and The Royal Family.

southwestern Sri Lanka. It is 7,559 feet
